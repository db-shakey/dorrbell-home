Same-Day Try On

Dorrbell is a same-day try on service that provides consumers with a convenient and efficient way to buy clothing and accessories. We've eliminated the inefficiencies and time-burn inherent in the in-store shopping model by bringing the fitting room to the customer.

Dorrbell's geo-aware marketplace gives the customer a real-time view of apparel inventory in nearby stores. Customers can select items from multiple stores and schedule an in-home try on appointment the same day. Dorrbell’s network of mobile shopping assistants deliver the items to the customer’s location in as little as an hour.

The customer can try on the items at home and only pay for what is kept. Dorrbell picks up the un-purchased items and returns them to their home retailer.

Fashion-Centric

Dorrbell partners exclusively with apparel retailers. Our platform is designed specifically for the nuanced logistics of fulfilling same-day try on requests for clothing and accessories.

Round-Trip Logistics

Dorrbell is not a same-day delivery service, but rather a same-day try on service. We don't just move boxes from point A to point B. We transport a fitting-room experience from retailers to their customers within a local market.

Same-day delivery companies transport products to a customer location, however, if the customer does not want to keep the product they must initiate a separate returns process.

Doorbell’s same-day try on service not only delivers products to the customer, but gives them the opportunity to physically experience the product prior to the purchase decision. Then, Dorrbell picks up un-purchased items, returns them to their home retailer... all in a single seamless transaction.

Try Before You Buy

Dorrbell customers do not have to buy the items before they are delivered. Payment happens after the in-home try on is complete and only for the items kept. Our approach recreates the in-store fitting room experience for the customer by allowing them to physically try on items without buying them first.
