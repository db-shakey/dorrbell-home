What Is Dorrbell?

Dorrbell allows people to actively shop for clothes from home and still enjoy the in-store benefits of seeing, touching and trying items on before buying.

How Dorrbell Works
Choose
Choose clothing from nearby stores to be delivered to your door.
Delivery
Schedule your delivery in as little as an hour or up to 5 days in advance.
Keep
Try your clothes and accessories on and decide what you want to keep.
Returns
We return to pick up the items you don't want. Pay only for what you keep.
Clothes Sold Locally At Your Fingertips
Dorrbell is synchronized with the inventories of your nearby stores so you know what products are available to experience at home. We're an extension of our retail partners' physical stores and transport merchandise between the store and your home without up-front payment.
Delivery & Returns Bundled Together
Not only do we bring the clothes you select to your door, but we also return to pick up the items you decide not to keep. We save you from the inconvenience and hassle of returning items by taking care of it for you and making it part of every order.
Surprise-Free Wardrobe Updates
Why wait for a box of items chosen by someone else to arrive at your doorstep when nobody knows what looks best on you better than you? Dorrbell gives you full control of your wardrobe updates and offers a try-before-buy experience without waiting for a shipment.
We Deliver The Fitting Room To Your Living Room
Dorrbell keeps you connected to the clothing stores in your area and informs you of new inventory, item markdowns and store-wide sales. Curate items over time or quickly assemble the fitting room items you want to try before buying. Either way, we'll deliver your personalized fitting room to your home at precisely the time you want so you can skip the trip to the store.
